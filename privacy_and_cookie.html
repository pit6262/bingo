Privacy and Cookie Policy

We are Matchbingo and this Privacy & Cookies policy (the ‘Policy’) describes the ways in which we collect and use your information (and information about you) when you visit our site and play our games on a browser, or through our app.

Matchbingo is committed to protecting the personal information entrusted to us by visitors to Matchbingo.co.uk and our players. We protect your personal information in accordance with anti-spam legislation and other applicable UK laws.

By playing our Games you are agreeing to be bound by this Policy in respect of the information collected about you. We may change this Policy at any time. If we decide to do so we shall notify you with pop-ups or banners when you enter or begin to use a Game, we shall deem your continued use of the Game as indicating your consent to such changes.

If you have any queries about this Policy, please get in touch with us using the contact details set out below and we will do our best to answer your questions.

Section A: What information we collect about you

  1. Personal Information Collected: We will collect the following personal information from you when you use our Games:
    1. your name;
    2. your job title;
    3. your e-mail address;
    4. your date of birth and other such information required to prove your age and identity (for example a scanned ID). We also use third party agencies to verify data in relation to your age and identity;
    5. your home address;
    6. your mobile telephone number;
    7. your credit card information and bank account details;
    8. information about your preferences and interests;
    9. information in relation to your purchases;
    10. information which we require to ensure compliance with and assist with enforcement of the site Terms.
    11. kany information you choose to provide us with if you contact us or via customer surveys;
    12. we may record your IP address, MAC address or the internet browser which you are using when you play Games; and
    13. information collected by our use of cookies and similar technologies (please see paragraphs 2 to 4 below).
  2. What are Cookies?: Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you play a Game. We use cookies when you play Games. Session cookies enable you to move from page to page within websites, Facebook or our Games and any information you enter will be remembered but is deleted when you close your browser or after a short time. Persistent cookies allow us to remember your preferences and settings when you play the Games in the future, in different browser sessions. Cookies record information about your online preferences and allow websites to tailor to your interests. Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings. To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, visit www.aboutcookies.org or www.allaboutcookies.org. To opt out of being tracked by Google Analytics across all websites visit http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
