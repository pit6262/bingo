How to play

What is Match Bingo

Matchbingo is a new concept that allows bingo to be played at sports events using real-time sports data to replace the traditional bingo ball. A bingo card is populated with actual events, as opposed to random numbers, which occur during a sports match. Bingo ticket sales (the stake) contribute into a prize pot and when the event outcome is concluded, the pot is won by a single person or shared amongst those who have the tickets with the winning outcome. Players pay a fixed ticket price, which is matched by all other participating players, and a percentage of the total amount of these stakes creates and determines the size of the prize pot.

The initial launch is utilising the data rich sport of Professional Darts to deliver our unique app-based bingo game. Players can participate at the live match, watching on TV or simply watching the results come through on their mobile phone.

Matchbingo Game Explained

Match Bingo creates a new fun way of watching sport or any event for that matter. Instead of cheering on either of the sides or celebrities players are hoping that their random event happens before anyone else can win the prize. The bingo card is populated with actual events, as opposed to random numbers, which occur during the sports match or event. Players can buy “cards” through our App and events are crossed off in the popular Bingo format, with prizes for the first player to complete each line. A Jackpot can be won should anyone complete the “Full House”.

Each Match Bingo card will display a random combination of events. For Darts Bingo there will be at least ? different combinations of throw outcomes or ‘events’ for each darts leg. The Match Bingo card will comprise the 15 boxes in a 5 x 3 Grid. Using a live data feed when a relevant event occurs it will be crossed off on the players behalf on their ‘Match Bingo Card’. With 3 individual winning horizontal lines and the Jackpot for the Full House.

Future sporting events Matchbingo aims to offer will range from cricket, football, basketball, motor racing etc. Entertainment events will also offer fun on Bingo lovers with games available for Love Island, favourite Soap Operas, Quiz shows and even Political Contests, etc.

How to Play

Playing on Matchbingo is just like playing on other Bingo games where you play with other participating players for a chance to win or share a variety of prize payouts.

It is simple to play:

  1. Select from the Sports event category that you wish to purchase bingo cards on.
  2. Scroll through the number of tickets to purchase and select your choice. There is a maximum number and a player can continue to purchase tickets until the maximum number is reached.
  3. Bingo Cards will be randomly generated and allocated. No card shall be exactly the same.
  4. Each card will comprise four games and prizes will be allocated to the first player/s to complete each of the three horizontal lines. In addition should anyone complete a full house (matching all 15 boxes) they can win the jackpot prize.
  5. If a match is abandoned then the prizes already won will be paid out and remaining funds returned to players on an equitable basis.
  6. Once players have confirmed their play, they will be sent a message to a notification inbox and for confirmation.
  7. Now go watch the games, or entertainment event and cheer on chosen teams or outcome.
  8. Once the event(s) about which participants have played MatchBingo on has concluded and results validated, winner(s) of the prizes will be notified and winning prize amounts will be automatically credited to their e-wallet. Where 2 or more players generate a simultaneous win then the prize is automatically shared equally.
